Install

Clippy installs from source into a per-user app folder, installs Electron dependencies, and starts the tray app.

curl -fsSL https://clippy.sh/install.sh | sh
irm https://clippy.sh/install.ps1 | iex

Prefer to inspect first? Download the script URL in your browser or with curl before running it.

Built for repeated use

Fast popup

Open at your cursor, search immediately, paste with Enter, and get back to what you were doing.

Numpad quick-paste

Assign your most-used snippets to 1-9 and paste them from anywhere.

Pins and groups

Keep durable snippets, tag them, and filter the list without turning your clipboard into a filing cabinet.

Native cloud sync

Uses cloud folders you may already have: Google Drive, OneDrive, and iCloud.

Text and images

Captures both clipboard text and images, with content-addressed image storage.

Open source

Electron app, local data files, no account, and no hosted clipboard database.
